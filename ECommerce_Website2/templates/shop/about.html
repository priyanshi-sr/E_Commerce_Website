My Super Cart

My Super Cart was founded in October 2007 in Bangalore[11] by Sachin Agarwal and Rohan Sharma, alumni of the IIT, Delhi and former Amazon employees.The company was started from a two-bedroom apartment in Kormangala, Bengaluru. The initial investment was provided by their families, which was INR 2 Lakh from each family. The website was kicked off in October 2007 and the company was only limited to selling books at that time.
